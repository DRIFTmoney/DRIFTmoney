Common questions

Everything you want to know before you open the app.

Is DRIFTmoney really free?
Yes, completely free. I built it for people like me who needed clarity without another subscription. If it helps you, there’s a tip jar — but it’s entirely optional, and the app is fully functional without it.
Does DRIFTmoney connect to my bank account?
No. DRIFTmoney never connects to your bank and never sees your real account data. Everything runs in your browser and saves locally to your device. Your financial information never leaves your computer. There’s no server, no database, no account — nothing to hack.
How is this different from YNAB, Mint, or other budget apps?
Most budget apps focus on categorizing past spending — they help you understand where your money went. DRIFTmoney focuses on the future — specifically, what your balance will look like on any given day based on your recurring bills and income. It answers “will I have enough money on the 15th?” not “where did my money go last month?” That’s a different question, and for a lot of people it’s the more useful one.
Do I need to log every purchase?
No. That’s the whole point. DRIFTmoney is built around recurring transactions — the bills and income you already know about. You enter those once and occasionally correct your running balance when reality diverges from the forecast. No transaction-by-transaction logging required.
Do I need to create an account?
No account, no email, no sign-up of any kind. Open the app, enter your information, and it saves automatically in your browser. Bookmark it and come back anytime.
What if I want to use it on multiple devices?
You can export your data as a JSON file with one click and import it on any other device. Simple backup and restore — no cloud accounts required.
Is this a good free alternative to YNAB?
DRIFTmoney and YNAB solve adjacent but different problems. YNAB is a full envelope budgeting system that requires significant setup and daily engagement. DRIFTmoney is a lightweight cash flow forecasting tool — less powerful, less work, and free. If you’ve tried YNAB and found it overwhelming, or if you just need to know “will I be okay this month,” DRIFTmoney might be exactly right.
