About DRIFTmoney

DRIFTmoney is a personal cash flow forecasting tool built for people who don't struggle with budgeting — they struggle with timing. Enter your recurring bills once, check your balance occasionally, and see exactly where your finances are headed over the coming weeks and months.

DRIFTmoney is a free, independent app developed and maintained by a solo developer. It runs entirely in your browser — no accounts, no servers, no data collection. Your financial data never leaves your device.

Pricing

DRIFTmoney is free and will stay free. No subscription, no upsell, no catch.

Refund Policy

Because DRIFTmoney is free to use, no charges are made for access to the application.

Voluntary tips are non-refundable. If you believe a tip was made in error, please contact me within 7 days at support@driftmoney.app and I will review your request on a case-by-case basis.

Cancellation Policy

There is nothing to cancel. DRIFTmoney has no subscription and no recurring charges. You can stop using the app at any time with no action required.

Because your data lives exclusively on your own device — never on any servers — there's no account to close and nothing stored anywhere that could follow you. If you'd like a clean slate, the Reset button in the app will permanently clear all of your local data. That's entirely in your hands, as it should be.
